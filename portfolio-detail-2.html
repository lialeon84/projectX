Project description

The first video is an application queue. I create this for my employer. They wanted a quick visual of the applications that customers were submitting online and how long the agents were taking to review the application. Whenever an application was over 8mins it would flash red. It was a very fun project.

The second video is a website I created for a client. She is an event creater, creating picnic's for her customers. She wanted away for her customers to send their request via online. I created a form for her with multiple selection options for customers to choose with the type of picnic they want and location. The form sends an email to the client with the customers request. The client was very happy with the results.
